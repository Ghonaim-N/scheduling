Availability Poll

This When2meet poll was created to coordinate our availability.

Instructions

1. Please enter your first name.

2. Enter a password if you would like the ability to return and modify your availability in the future (optional).

3. Click "Sign In" to bring you to an editable calendar.

4. Click and drag across the grid to highlight the time slots you are free. These slots will turn green.

When2Meet Direct Link

The results for this poll will be used to find times to schedule a meeting. Times will be chosen that best fit into all schedules.
